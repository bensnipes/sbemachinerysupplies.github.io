Services We Offer

We can save you your precious time, money and improve the general health of your machines.

Undercarriage Replacement

Our experienced technical team first conducts an undercarriage wear analyses to determine which components need replacement. For the components that are in good conditions, we help you to estimate the remaining life to avoid unaware breakdowns.

GETs & Bucket Replacement

The GETs we supply have been tested and proven economical for both construction and mining purposes. Upon request, we are able to customize the GETs to suit the particular abrasive rock and soil formations for efficient operations.

Machine Diagnostics

Our team is well-trained and experienced making them the go-to-solution for excavator owners in the industry. With over a decade and half experience in the industry, we have carved a niche for ourselves as the best machine diagnostic service provider.
